Hello Doctors,

We are excited to work closely with you to optimize the ophthalmic health of your patient. If you decide to refer a patient to us for diagnosis or treatment, please be assured that we will emphasize your role as the patient's primary provider. We are interested in the medical side of ophthalmic care, and we would like to co-manage patients whenever possible.


Following your patient's visit to our office, you will receive a letter detailing their history, exam findings, and our impression/plan for your patient. In-depth imaging of your patient will accompany our correspondence with you.

In order to provide access to all patients, we will accept all patients, regardless of insurance status. Our staff will work with your patients to get them insurance.

We are always available for you and your patients, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Please contact us anytime at 718-585-5500.

Dr. Neil Patel, MD

Dr. Neil S. Patel is the Founder and President of both New York Ophthalmology. He is is a vitreoretinal specialist and is affiliated with the New York Eye and Ear Infirmary, Mt. Sinai Hospital, and St. Barnabas Hospital.

Dr. Patel is board-certified and a Diplomate of the American Board of Ophthalmology, and is Fellow of the American Academy of Ophthalmology. He has numerous publications in peer-reviewed journals and has experience lecturing nationally and internationally. When not in the office, Dr. Patel enjoys exercising and in an avid chess player

Dr. Xining He, MD

Dr. Xining He practices comprehensive ophthalmology and medical retina. Dr. He graduated from Yale College where she received her BA in mathematics and economics. She obtained her medical degree from Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. Dr. He completed her internship in internal medicine and surgery at New York Hospital of Queens. She then completed her residency training in ophthalmology at Yale-New Haven Hospital and the Yale Eye Center. She then went on to complete a fellowship in medical retina at Moorfields Eye Hospital in London. Dr. He was briefly a reporter for a medical blog.

Dr. Justin Rapp, OD

Dr. Justin Rapp is a graduate of SUNY Binghamton University and obtained his doctoral degree from SUNY College of Optometry. Dr. Rapp's clinical experience exposed him to a diverse array of areas ranging from Ocular Disease, Vision Therapy, Pediatrics, Geriatrics, and Specialty Contact Lenses. He received advanced training in Primary Care and Ocular Disease, completing externships at the Tucson VA Medical Center and OMNI Surgical Center in Atlanta. Dr. Rapp is a primary eye care physician with experience treating a wide range of medical and visual eye problems. When not in the office, he can probably be found at the Crossfit gym or cooking up something delicious.

Dr. Gary Steinberg, OD

Dr. Steinberg is a native of Pittsburgh and graduate of Touro College. He received a Doctorate in Optometry from Pennsylvania College of Optometry in Philadelphia. Dr. Steinberg provides primary eye care with an emphasis in the diagnosis and treatment of ocular disease at the Bronx office as well as numerous satellite clinics in New York City. Dr. Steinberg enjoys spending time with his family at home in New Jersey and is an avid reader of science fiction novels.
